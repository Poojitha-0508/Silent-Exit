---

## Finding 1 — Recency is the #1 Churn Driver

| Recency Bucket | Churn Rate |
|----------------|------------|
| 0–30 days | 16.47% |
| 31–90 days | 15.71% |
| 91–180 days | 24.48% |
| 181–365 days | 40.40% |
| 365+ days | **54.48%** |

**Insight:** Customers inactive for 365+ days churn at 54.48% — nearly 3x higher than recent buyers at 16.47%.

**Recommendation:**
- Trigger automated re-engagement email/SMS at **90 days of inactivity**
- Offer personalized discount at **180 days** to win them back
- Flag **365+ day** inactive customers as lost and attempt win-back campaign

---

## Finding 2 — Membership Tier Directly Controls Churn

| Tier | Churn Rate |
|------|------------|
| None | 53.08% |
| Silver | 38.66% |
| Gold | 30.15% |
| Platinum | 23.68% |

**Insight:** None-tier customers churn at 2.24x the rate of Platinum members. Every tier upgrade significantly reduces churn.

**Recommendation:**
- Offer free Silver trial after 2nd purchase for None-tier customers
- Show None-tier customers benefits they are missing
- Target high-spend None-tier customers for Gold upgrade

---

## Finding 3 — Churn is Growing Rapidly

| Year | Churned Customers |
|------|------------------|
| 2021 | 1,500 |
| 2022 | 6,800 |
| 2023 | 14,000 |
| 2024 (Jan–Jun) | 11,000 |

**Insight:** Churn grew 9x from 2021 to 2023 — a systemic retention crisis that is accelerating.

**Recommendation:**
- Set up monthly churn rate alert — trigger if churn exceeds threshold
- Investigate product/pricing changes during 2022–2023 spike
- Implement proactive retention strategy, not reactive

---

## Finding 4 — 9,069 Customers Need Immediate Attention

| Risk Tier | Customers | % of Base | Revenue |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| High Risk | 9,069 | 10.5% | ₹9.84M |
| Medium Risk | 38,931 | 44.5% | ₹758.84M |
| Low Risk | 41,075 | 45% | ₹1.15B |

**Insight:** High Risk is only 10.5% of base but holds ₹9.84M in revenue. Medium Risk holds ₹758M — the bigger opportunity.

**Recommendation:**
- Immediate outreach to all 9,069 High Risk customers
- Medium Risk nurture campaign — these hold 10x more revenue
- Prioritize high-spend customers within each risk tier

---

## Finding 5 — Demographics Do NOT Drive Churn

| Dimension | Churn Rate Range | Variation |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------|
| Device | 37.02% – 38.01% | <1% |
| Age Group | 37.01% – 38.87% | <2% |
| Gender | 36.76% – 37.62% | <1% |
| Channel | 36% – 38% | <2% |

**Insight:** Churn is uniform across all demographic segments. Device, age, gender, and channel do NOT predict churn.

**Recommendation:**
- Do not build demographic-specific retention strategies
- All budget should go to **recency-based engagement**
- Focus on WHEN customers buy, not WHO they are
